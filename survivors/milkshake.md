Name: Milkshake
Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ 
Survivor Type: Support

Abilities:

→ Soundblast (Q Keybind)
Milkshake takes out her megaphone and shouts towards the killer
- If the hit is successful: Killer gets Blindness II for 2 seconds and Slowness I for 1 second
- If the hit is not successful: Milkshake is punished with 0.8s endlag
- Charges: Successful hit adds +1 charge
- Maximum Charge Limit: 3
- Cooldown: 28s (successful), 20s (unsuccessful)

→ Field Aid (E Keybind)
Milkshake takes out her medkit and bandages with a 1.5s wind up, patching up the nearest survivor.
- Requirement: 2 charges
- Target: Closest survivor to Milkshake
- Effect: Heals 20 HP and gives Regenerate II for 4 seconds
- Cast Time: 6 seconds (using the medkit)
- Charge Use: Consumes 3 charges

-> Syringe (R Keybind)
Milkshake uses a syringe filled with adrenaline.
- Requirement: 3 charges
- Range Check:
- If there is no survivor within 10 studs, Milkshake injects herself and there is a 0.4 second endlag.
- Effect: +10 HP and Speed II for 2 seconds
- Risk: Milkshake gets Blindness I for 1 second
- If there is a survivor within 10 studs, Milkshake injects that survivor without any endlag.
- Effect: +10 HP and Speed II for 2 seconds
- Cast Time: 0.7 seconds
- Charge Use: Consumes 3 charges
