Name: Milkshake
Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ 
Survivor Type: Support

Abilities:

-> Soundblast (Q Keybind)
Milkshake takes out her megaphone and shouts very loud towards the killer, if the hit is succesful then the killer gets Blindness II for 2 seconds and Slowness I for 1 second, if the hit isn't succesful Milkshake is punished by a 0.8 seconds endlag.

This ability also has charges, if the hit is succesful it will add +1 to the charges. You can use these charges to use other abilities.
Maximum Charge Limit: 10

Cooldown: 26s if the hit is succesful, 18s if the hit is unsuccesful.
